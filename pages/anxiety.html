Anxiety doesn't have to run your life

A structured six-week programme that gives you real tools. Not just talking — measured progress, practical skills, and a clinician in your corner.

The pathway

Week 1: Assessment & Goal Setting

  • Clinical assessment and baseline GAD-7
  • Goal setting for the six-week programme
  • Psychoeducation about anxiety

Week 2: CBT Foundations

  • Understanding the anxiety cycle
  • Thought challenging techniques
  • Identifying triggers and patterns

Week 3: Mid-Point Review

  • Repeat GAD-7 to measure progress
  • Behavioural activation and exposure planning
  • Review what's working and adjust

Week 4: Practical Skills

  • Grounding and breathing techniques
  • Cognitive restructuring in real situations
  • Building confidence through practice

Week 5: Relapse Prevention

  • Building a personal toolkit
  • Recognising early warning signs
  • Planning for challenging situations

Week 6: Final Review & Discharge

  • Final GAD-7 and progress review
  • Discharge summary to GP
  • Plan for maintaining gains

Is this right for you?

✓ Who this is for

  • Generalised anxiety affecting daily function
  • Social anxiety impacting work, study, or relationships
  • Panic symptoms
  • Worry and rumination that won't switch off
  • Mild to moderate severity — not currently in crisis

✗ Who this is not for

  • Active suicidal crisis (call or text 1737)
  • Severe mental illness requiring psychiatric inpatient care
  • Active substance dependence as primary presentation
  • Already engaged in secondary mental health services
