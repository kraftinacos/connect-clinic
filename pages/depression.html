Feeling flat doesn't mean you're broken

A structured six-week programme to help you get moving again. Measured outcomes, practical tools, and a clinician who actually listens.

The pathway

Week 1: Assessment & Goal Setting

  • Clinical assessment and baseline PHQ-9
  • Goal setting for the six-week programme
  • Psychoeducation about depression

Week 2: Behavioural Activation

  • Small steps approach
  • Rebuilding routine and momentum
  • Activity scheduling

Week 3: Mid-Point Review

  • Repeat PHQ-9 to measure progress
  • Cognitive work — identifying negative thought patterns
  • Challenging unhelpful thinking

Week 4: Values & Meaning

  • Reconnecting with what matters to you
  • Values-based goal setting
  • Building motivation through meaning

Week 5: Relapse Prevention

  • Building sustainable habits
  • Recognising early warning signs
  • Creating a wellness plan

Week 6: Final Review & Discharge

  • Final PHQ-9 and progress review
  • Discharge summary to GP
  • Plan for maintaining gains

Is this right for you?

✓ Who this is for

  • Mild to moderate depression affecting daily function
  • Persistent low mood and loss of interest
  • Fatigue and reduced motivation
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
  • Not currently in crisis or actively suicidal

✗ Who this is not for

  • Active suicidal crisis (call or text 1737)
  • Severe depression with psychotic features
  • Requires psychiatric inpatient care
  • Active substance dependence as primary presentation
