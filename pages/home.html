Who is this for?

Our services are designed for adults across Aotearoa seeking professional mental health support.

Adults 18+ +

Our services are designed for adults. If you're under 18, please speak with your GP or school counsellor about youth mental health services.

New Zealand residents +

We provide services to anyone currently in Aotearoa New Zealand, including citizens, permanent residents, and those on valid visas.

Private paying clients +

We operate as a private clinic. Our fees are transparent and payment is required at the time of booking. We do not currently bulk bill.

Those seeking ADHD clarity +

If you've always felt different, struggled with focus, or suspect you might have ADHD, our comprehensive assessment can provide answers.

People managing anxiety +

If anxiety is affecting your daily life, work, or relationships, our structured programme can help you develop effective coping strategies.

Those experiencing depression +

If you're feeling persistently low, unmotivated, or hopeless, our compassionate support can help you find your way back to wellness.
