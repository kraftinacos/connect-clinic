Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Do I need a referral to book an appointment? +

No, you don't need a referral to see us. You can book directly through our website. However, if you have a regular GP, we recommend letting them know you're seeking mental health support, as they can be involved in your ongoing care if needed.

How do virtual consultations work? +

All our consultations are conducted via secure video call. Once your booking is confirmed, you'll receive a link to join the video session at your appointment time. You'll need a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone) and a stable internet connection. We use encrypted, HIPAA-compliant platforms to ensure your privacy.

Can I get medication through Connect Clinic? +

Yes, our psychiatrists can prescribe medication when clinically appropriate. For ongoing prescriptions, you'll need a GP who can continue prescribing under our guidance. If you don't have a GP, we can provide guidance on finding one.

How long does the ADHD assessment take? +

Our ADHD assessment involves three sessions over 3-4 weeks. Session 1 is 90 minutes, Session 2 is 60 minutes, and Session 3 is 45 minutes. This allows us to conduct a thorough assessment and provide you with a comprehensive report and treatment plan.

What if I'm not sure which service I need? +

That's completely fine. When booking, select "Not sure - help me decide" and we'll discuss your situation in a general consultation. We can help determine the most appropriate pathway for your needs. You can also email us with any questions before booking.

Do you offer services for people under 18? +

Our services are designed for adults aged 18 and over. If you're under 18, we recommend speaking with your GP or school counsellor, who can refer you to appropriate youth mental health services in your area.

Can I claim the cost through my health insurance? +

We don't currently bulk bill, but you may be able to claim a portion of the cost back through your health insurance. Coverage varies between providers and plans, so please check with your insurer about psychiatric consultation coverage.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We understand that circumstances change. We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may incur a fee of 50% of the consultation cost. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.

Is everything I share confidential? +

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of our practice. Everything you share with us is kept private and secure. The only exceptions are if there's a serious risk of harm to yourself or others, or if we're legally required to disclose information. We'll always discuss this with you first where possible.

Do you provide services for people outside New Zealand? +

We are only able to provide services to people currently in New Zealand. This is because our clinicians are registered to practice in Aotearoa and governed by New Zealand medical regulations.

What happens after I submit a booking request? +

After you submit your booking request, we'll review your information and respond within 24-48 hours. We'll either confirm your booking with available appointment times, or contact you if we need more information. Once you confirm a time, you'll receive instructions for payment and your video consultation link.

What makes Connect Clinic different? +

Connect Clinic is Pacific-led and committed to culturally responsive care. We combine specialist psychiatric expertise with genuine compassion. Being fully virtual means we can reach people across all of Aotearoa, removing geographical barriers to quality mental health care. Our clinicians are Fellows of the Royal Australian and New Zealand College of Psychiatrists with extensive experience.
