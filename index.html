Dr Francis Katoa

GP, RNZCGP Fellow

Raised in a traditional Tongan household in South Auckland, Dr Katoa brings seven years of frontline primary care experience to Connect Clinic. A Fellow of the Royal New Zealand College of General Practitioners with specialised training in CBT, youth mental health, and cultural safety, he built Connect Clinic because too many young Pasifika and Māori were falling through the cracks of a system that wasn't designed for them.

He serves as Secretary of the RNZCGP Pacific Chapter and Clinical Skills Tutor at the University of Auckland.
