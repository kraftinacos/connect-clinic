The Missing Middle Connect
Anxiety, depression, stress, ADHD, grief, sleep. Where most young people sit. Where the system has its largest gap.
Primary Care
15-minute GP consults. No structured pathways. No outcome tracking.

The Auditor-General found that services for young people are fragmented, and not all have timely access to appropriate care. Connect sits in the gap — structured, virtual, outcome-measured intervention delivered before crisis.

How It Works

Four steps. Six sessions. Measured outcomes.

About

Built inside the system it aims to fix

Connect Clinic is led by Dr Francis Katoa — GP, RNZCGP Fellow, seven years in high-needs primary care across Aotearoa. Trained in CBT, youth and adolescent mental health, and medicinal cannabis prescribing.

This is not a startup. This is a clinician who has worked in the under-resourced clinics, seen young people cycle through fragmented services, and built what the system does not provide.

Connect operates under Pasifika Rise Limited. Pacific-governed. Aotearoa-based.
