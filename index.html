About

Connect is led by Dr Francis Katoa — GP, RNZCGP Fellow, seven years in high-needs primary care. Trained in CBT and youth mental health.

Connect exists because the system has a gap. In 2025, UNICEF ranked New Zealand last out of 36 wealthy nations for youth mental health. The youth suicide rate is 17.1 per 100,000 — nearly three times the OECD average. Māori youth rates are double non-Māori. Over the past five years, 10,000 fewer young people have accessed mental health services, even as distress has risen.

The Auditor-General found that services for young people are fragmented. Most sit in the missing middle — too complex for a 15-minute GP consult, not severe enough for specialist care, unable to afford private therapy. Connect provides structured, virtual, outcome-measured intervention in that gap.

Connect operates under Pasifika Rise Limited. Pacific-governed. Aotearoa-based.
